## Synthesized research findings

1. Production brand identity work needs staged deliverables: discovery brief, concept exploration, logo system, color/type system, usage rules, export assets, and real-world usage tests.
2. AI logo workflows are strongest when AI is used for exploration, prompt iteration, mockups, and variant discovery, while final production needs human critique, vector cleanup, spacing/lockup rules, contrast checks, and export validation.
3. Media Studio UX should preserve prompt history, provider/model choices, variants, critique notes, approval state, cost/usage, asset provenance, and where-used tracking.
4. A FAMtastic logo workflow must separate Logo Lab from generic hero/image generation. A logo needs vectorization, lockups, favicon/app-icon tests, dark/light tests, animation rules, and brand-kit promotion.
5. Animation/React effects belong in the design language as motion rules and later UI prototypes, not as a blocker for the first production logo package.

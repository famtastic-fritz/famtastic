- FAMtastic Designs is the current build focus and should not be confused with FAMtastic Hosting or FAMtastic Thoughts.
- FAMtastic Hosting already exists and is collecting payments; it can be cross-promoted and used where useful.

- FAMtastic Church Connect is a campaign/product under FAMtastic Designs, not a separate business.
- FAMtastic Designs should sell transformation, not just websites.
- The core offer is proof-first: show prospects what they could become, let them claim/pay, then launch/grow/automate.
- The first three paid service areas are small business websites, AI automation, and mobile-first e-commerce/business systems.
- The first revenue engine should be one shared FAMtastic Proof Engine with multiple campaign skins.
- Initial campaigns should include churches/ministries, nonprofits/community organizations, local service businesses, and professional/service providers.
- Churches are a distinctive thesis because they collect money weekly and can benefit from giving, streaming, events, membership, and visitor follow-up.
- FAMtastic should not put all early revenue dependency into churches; it should track 3-4 categories in parallel.
- The site should be a FAMtastic Designs Foundation MVP, not a throwaway landing page.
- The site should be immersive throughout, not just a normal site with a 3D hero.
- The site needs navigation, footer, contact/business review form, page creation logic, backend logic, private previews, and design consistency rules.
- The business should be automation-first with human exception handling because Fritz cannot manually operate the funnel every day.
- AI quality review should happen first, with Fritz reviewing only flagged cases. Later high-confidence proofs can auto-send.
- Outreach must be dynamic and based on observed facts about each prospect.
- Email sending should use dedicated FAMtastic Designs addresses, not Fritz’s personal Gmail.
- GoDaddy/Workspace-style email may be used for inboxes and human replies.
- Resend may be used for automated proof delivery, follow-ups, confirmations, and event tracking.
- Deliverability/compliance guardrails are required: SPF, DKIM, DMARC, suppression, unsubscribe/opt-out, duplicate checks, source tracking, and compliance checks.
- Pricing should remain flexible and dynamic, not rigidly tied to a single package.

## 4. Honest caveats (do not skip)

- **The amount-in-link is not guaranteed.** The billing capability renders
  `https://cash.app/<cashtag>/<amount>`, but Cash App does **not** officially support an
  amount path segment — `cash.app/$cashtag` reliably opens your profile, but the pre-filled
  amount may not populate. For a guaranteed pre-filled amount, generate a **Cash App Payment
  Link from inside the app** (Request → share link) and paste it into the invoice as
  `method: "link"` instead. Always state the amount + invoice number in the message text as a
  backstop. ([Cash App: what is a cashtag](https://cash.app/help/3123-what-is-a-cashtag), [Cash App payment links](https://cash.app/press/cash-app-launches-payment-links))
- **Cash App receiving limit if unverified.** Unverified accounts can only receive ~**$1,000
  per rolling 30 days** (total account cap ~$1,500); verified accounts have effectively
  unlimited receiving. $100 is fine either way, but if you'll stack several payments,
  **verify your identity first** (free, minutes). ([Cash App account limits](https://cash.app/help/6543-account-limits), [Wealthvieu](https://wealthvieu.com/banking/cash-app/cash-app-limits/))
- **New-account / unusual-payment holds.** First-time or unusual inflows can land as **"pending
  for review,"** typically clearing in **24–72 hours** (rarely up to 10 business days). Receiving
  from a *known* contact on an established account is least likely to be held — another reason
  the owed-invoice play (#1) and warm-network play (#3) beat selling to strangers today.
  ([LegalClarity](https://legalclarity.org/does-cash-app-hold-funds-and-how-long-does-it-last/), [Cash App: payment pending](https://cash.app/help/us/en-us/3042-payment-pending))
- **A buyer must actually exist.** The website/logo/consult plays only pay today if a real,
  reachable person with $100 says yes today. Spend your first 10 minutes identifying that
  named human, not building. If none exists for the sell plays, fall back to #1.
- **Domain flips and recurring care plans are NOT today money** — marketplace payouts are
  ~5+ business days and recurring deals have weeks-long cycles. Keep them off today's board.

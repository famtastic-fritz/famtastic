# Session 111 — 2026-03-24

Before I implement, I need you to make a few key calls so the popup actually works for your business:

**1. Timing — When should it appear?**
- Immediately on page load (grabs attention first)
- After 10-15 seconds (lets them see hero first)
- Exit intent (last chance as they're leaving)

**2. What's the core ask?**
- "Get Your Free Lawn Assessment"
- "Schedule a Consultation" 
- "Book Your Spring Cleanup"
- Something else?

**3. What's the urgency hook?**
- Seasonal: "Spring season is filling up — book now"
- Limited offer: "Free quote + lawn analysis"
- Simple: "Ready to transform your lawn?"

**4. Size/feel?**
- Centered modal (clean, balanced)
- Corner card (less intrusive)
- Full-screen (bold, premium)

**5. CTA destination?**
- Link to contact form?
- Phone number to call?
- Email signup?

Give me those five decisions and I'll build it into the home page—fully styled, branded, with a smooth close button so it feels premium not pushy.

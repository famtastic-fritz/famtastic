# FAMtastic Designs — Business Model & Offer Architecture

> Productizing the FAMtastic site factory into a repeatable revenue engine.
>
> Generated by the Agent Factory · 2026-06-18 · internal use only


## The one-line model
We sell *confidently-different* websites — produced by the FAMtastic factory in
days, not months — through a value ladder that lands a small paid win first,
then upgrades it into a full build and a recurring care plan.

## Why this wins
- **Production cost is near-zero and falling.** The factory already builds
  multi-page, motion-rich sites from a brief. Our marginal cost per site is
  hours of review, not weeks of design labor. That is the arbitrage.
- **We lead with a paid diagnostic, not a free pitch.** A small paid first step
  qualifies the buyer, funds outreach, and creates the artifact that closes the
  bigger sale.
- **Every delivery produces a reusable asset** (before/after, Loom, template),
  which compounds into proof for the next sale.

### Core build tiers
- **Launch ($1,500):** 3-page FAMtastic site, 1 hero vocabulary, contact form,
  basic SEO, deploy. The factory's default output, reviewed and shipped.
- **Signature ($3,500):** 5–7 pages, custom hero + character branding, motion,
  copywriting pass, analytics + lead capture wired, 1 revision round.
- **Flagship ($7,500):** Everything in Signature + booking/payment integration,
  multi-vertical content, A/B-ready hero variants, 30-day post-launch tuning.

## Unit economics (per Signature build, conservative)
- Price: **$3,500**
- Factory + review cost (compute + ~4h human review @ blended $50/h): ~**$250**
- Outreach cost to land it (CAC at 2% reply→close on paid outreach): ~**$300**
- **Contribution margin: ~$2,950 (84%)** before the recurring plan.
- Add care plan at $129/mo → **~$1,548 ARR** per client on top.

## The "diagnostic funds the machine" loop
1. Sell a $500 diagnostic. 2. Deliver in 48h with a Loom. 3. Credit the $500
toward a build if they hire within 7 days. 4. The build funds the next 10
outreach batches. 5. The care plan turns one-time cash into MRR. This is the
flywheel — paid at every step so the pipeline never runs on hope.

## Revenue targets (first 90 days, deliberately modest)
- Month 1: 4 diagnostics ($2,000) + 1 build ($3,500) = **$5,500**
- Month 2: 6 diagnostics + 2 builds + 3 care plans = **~$11,400**
- Month 3: convert backlog → 4 builds + 8 care plans = **~$18,000 + $1,032 MRR**

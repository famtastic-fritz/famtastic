Questions, answered

Is the free tier crippled?

No. The free tier produces correct, complete, shippable tags and a real share image. Paid tiers add branding, watermark removal, and bulk — not the basics.

Do I need an account to try it?

No. The tool at / works with zero signup. You only create an account when you subscribe to Pro or Agency.

Where does my data go?

Generation runs in your browser. On the free tier nothing is stored server-side. Brand presets on paid plans are saved to your account so they sync across devices.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, self-serve. You keep access until the end of the billing period. Downgrading drops you to Free with all core features intact.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes — annual is billed at 10× the monthly rate (roughly two months free). Pro $90/yr, Agency $290/yr.

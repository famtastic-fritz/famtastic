For **premium brand studio** interfaces in React, the strongest animation approach is usually:

## Best-fit patterns
1. **Micro-interactions**
   - Button hover/press states
   - Card lift, focus rings, icon feedback
   - Form validation and tooltip transitions  
   **Why:** polished without distracting from the brand content.

2. **Editorial page transitions**
   - Hero reveal, section fade/slide, staggered text
   - Route transitions between case studies or campaign pages  
   **Why:** feels high-end and cinematic.

3. **Content choreography**
   - Staggered grids, image + copy sequencing, scroll-triggered reveals
   - Animated composition for portfolios and launches  
   **Why:** ideal for brand/storytelling sites.

4. **Gesture-driven UI**
   - Drag galleries, image carousels, interactive cards  
   **Why:** adds “crafted” feel on premium experiences.

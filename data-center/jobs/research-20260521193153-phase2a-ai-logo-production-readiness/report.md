## Recommended workflow
1. **Strategy first**
   - Define brand positioning, audience, tone, and use cases before prompting.
   - AI is best for **exploration**, not deciding the brand story.  
   Source: House of GAI emphasizes AI should assist research/variation, not core creative direction or brand strategy [2].

2. **Prompt for concept exploration**
   - Use AI to generate many directions quickly: symbols, metaphors, visual styles, and rough mark ideas.
   - Good for “20 variations of a softer shield” or “speed without wings,” rather than final logos.  
   Source: YouTube workflow advice on using AI as a sketchbook/concept tool [7].

3. **Move to vector-native tools for production**
   - Prefer tools that output or build **true vectors/SVG**, not just raster images.
   - Recraft is highlighted for native SVG outputs; Logo Diffusion claims true vector logo construction rather than tracing [3][1].

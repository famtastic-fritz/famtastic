## One-page sales doc (publishable)
**Checkout / Conversion Rescue Diagnostic — $500 flat, 48 hours**
- **Who it's for:** merchants or agencies with a live checkout/funnel that's
  leaking completed orders.
- **What you get:**
  1. Full checkout-flow audit (every step, every drop-off).
  2. Gateway / plugin / integration review (Authorize.Net, Stripe, WooCommerce,
     Shopify — wherever the money flows).
  3. Fraud / risk-rule check (false declines kill revenue too).
  4. Tokenization / recurring-billing check where relevant.
  5. A prioritized fix plan (what to fix first, expected impact).
  6. A Loom walkthrough so your team can act without a meeting.
- **Turnaround:** 48 hours from payment + access.
- **CTA:** "Send me your store URL and I'll start today."
- **Guarantee:** if the findings aren't worth the $500, I'll say so — you keep
  the full report regardless.

## Upsell mechanics
- The $500 diagnostic **credits in full** toward an implementation sprint if they
  hire within 7 days. This makes "yes" to the diagnostic nearly free in their
  head and pre-frames the bigger sale.
- After any build, offer the care plan as the default ("keep it fast, secure,
  and updated for $129/mo") — opt-out, not opt-in.

## Objection handling
- *"$500 is a lot for an audit."* → "It's credited toward the fix, and you keep
  the report either way. The leak is costing more than $500/month — that's the
  point."
- *"We'll just look ourselves."* → "You can — the diagnostic is the 48-hour,
  prioritized version your team would take two weeks to produce, with a fix plan
  attached."
- *"Can you guarantee more sales?"* → "I guarantee a complete diagnosis and a
  prioritized plan. The fixes move the number; I'll show you the expected
  impact, ranked."

## Definition of a closed sale
Verbal/email "yes" → PayPal invoice sent → **invoice paid** → access received →
delivery clock starts. "Paid" is the only status that counts.

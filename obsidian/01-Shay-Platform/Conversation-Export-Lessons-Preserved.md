Title: Conversation Export Lessons Preserved
Purpose: Preserve the durable lessons and operating truth from this proof lane so they are not lost even if the active session or messy working state disappears.

What happened
- This lane started as a recall/conversation-export proof case.
- The work was pushed through an adversarial outer loop.
- Last confirmed outer-loop index: 15.
- During the work, a second internal continuation loop was effectively created to keep the lane from dying when interruption risk became obvious.

What was proven
1. Search-heavy recall is real
- The answer could be recovered from the current system.
- The current path depends too much on search and reasoning instead of a direct answer surface.

2. Raw transcript capture is not the real target
- Existing capture/review surfaces already preserve a trail.
- The actual upgrade target is continuity, restart, traceability, and cheaper future reuse.

3. Plans keep failing at n instead of n+1
- A plan can look good and still fail operationally if it does not define how the work continues, hands off, checkpoints, or closes without Fritz.
- Plan completeness must include the autonomy step, not just the work step.

4. Nested loops create motion without closure
- Outer loop: adversarial review loop.
- Inner loop: self-preservation / checkpoint / continuation loop.
- Without an explicit closure rule, loops can keep producing activity while still failing the real objective.

5. Eval/proof work is too close to live truth
- The lane revealed that evaluation/proof activity can bleed too close to live main truth.
- That means lane separation and promotion boundaries are not strong enough yet.

Operating truth earned
- Proof/exploration work belongs in a branch-proof lane first.
- Main brain should receive distilled truth later, not raw active discovery.
- Main branch should only receive milestone truth after the lesson is stable and properly scoped.

What must be preserved from this lane
- Last confirmed outer-loop index: 15
- The n+1 lesson: plans must include continuation/handoff/closeout
- The nested-loop lesson: motion is not closure
- The promotion-boundary lesson: branch-proof truth and main truth must stay separate until distilled

Promotion rule from this point
- Preserve active discovery on the branch.
- Distill lessons into named packets/checkpoints.
- Promote only the stable milestone truth later.

Files that now carry this preservation
- Conversation-Export-Traceable-Intelligence-Brief.md
- Conversation-Export-Loop-Checkpoint-Now.md
- Conversation-Export-Branch-Lessons-01.md
- Conversation-Export-Lessons-Preserved.md

Next clean move
- Define the exact boundary between branch-proof truth and main-brain truth.
- Do not force premature promotion just to feel organized.

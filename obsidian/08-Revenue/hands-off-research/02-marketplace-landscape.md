# Hands-Off Income — Marketplace Landscape (agent 2 of 3)
> Overnight swarm, 2026-06-05. Raw. Synthesized into FRITZ-HANDS-OFF-INCOME-PLAY.md.

## The core trap
**"Build it and they come" FAILS on a standalone site, WORKS only on real-discovery marketplaces.** Two buckets:
- **Real built-in discovery** — buyers browse/search *inside* the platform; you can win without outside marketing.
- **Storefront-only** ("marketplace" in name) — checkout, not customers; you still do the marketing.

## Ranked channels
| Channel | Buyers come to you? | Passive $ (indie) | AI-automatable? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Chrome Web Store** | ✅ YES (~40% installs organic store search; Google surfaces extensions) | $500–5k/mo niche; outliers $15–42k/mo | HIGH (crank 5–10 targeted extensions) |

## TOP 3 where buyers genuinely come to YOU
1. **Chrome Web Store** — strongest "Play Store" analog; Google's browser IS the distribution; AI-buildable extension portfolio; $5–20k/mo ceiling for ~10 extensions. Freemium + $5–15/mo via ExtensionPay/Stripe.
2. **Shopify App Store** — highest-intent buyers (merchants with credit cards + urgent problems); 85% rev share to $1M; ~$93k/yr avg. Harder build (OAuth/billing/review).
3. **iOS/Google Play (small-app portfolio)** — the literal "Play Store" model; one indie did 30 apps → $22k/mo in <1yr; ASO does discovery. Review latency + native overhead = slower iteration.

**Honest:** Gumroad/GPT-Store/Envato-Elements = you're still the marketing dept (or no payout). The real built-in discovery is Chrome Web Store, the app stores, Shopify, and (for design assets) Creative Market.

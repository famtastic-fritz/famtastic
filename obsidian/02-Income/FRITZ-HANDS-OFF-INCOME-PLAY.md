# Fritz's Hands-Off Income Play (2026-06-05)

> Synthesis of a 3-agent overnight swarm (repos · marketplace landscape · tailored play). Raw reports
> in `obsidian/08-Revenue/hands-off-research/`. Built for exactly who you are: hands-off, no outreach,
> store-distributed, automation does the work, buyers come to you. Honest — no hype.

## TL;DR — the ONE move (so you don't scatter)
**This week: list 3–5 websites you've ALREADY built on Creative Market as HTML templates.**
Your Site Studio is already the factory — this is a *packaging* job, not a new build. Shay/Claude Code
package them; you make one seller account and upload. First dollar in 2–4 weeks. Everything else below
queues *behind* this one action.

## The honest truth (the trap, named)
"Build it and they come" **fails on a standalone site** and **works only on marketplaces with real
built-in discovery** (buyers browse/search *inside* the platform). Storefronts like Gumroad and the
GPT Store give you checkout, not customers — you'd still be the marketing department. So the whole game
is **picking channels that bring the buyers for you.**

## Your stack — in sequence, not as a buffet
| Priority | Play | Why | Ceiling |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. START** | **Creative Market HTML templates**, built by Site Studio | Reuses your #1 asset; fastest first dollar; real design-buyer traffic; zero support (download & deploy) | $800–2,500/mo @ 10–20 templates in 12mo |
| **2. SCALE** | **Chrome Web Store extension portfolio** | The *truest* "Play Store" model — strongest built-in discovery, AI-buildable — but a NEW product line, so it's next, not now | $5–20k/mo @ ~10 extensions |
| **3. UPSELL** | **$299 auto-delivered "custom site from your brief"** (Typeform → Shay → Gumroad) | Productized *service*, not SaaS (no churn/support); the path to your "1,000 @ $100/mo" north star | $6k/mo @ 20 orders/mo |
| **4. BACKGROUND** | Your **content engine** niche sites | Already built; pure passive — but 9–18mo to rank + Google HCU risk. Let it compound quietly | $300–1.5k/mo Year 2 |
| **5. FUNNEL** | Free **Site Studio Claude plugin** (`/site-studio:build`) | Not direct income — a free brand/audience funnel that drives buyers to #1 and #3 | indirect |

**Why Creative Market leads over the higher-ceiling Chrome/Shopify plays:** those need a *new* product
built from scratch (extensions, Shopify apps). Creative Market sells the product your factory *already
makes*. Bank wins from what exists first; build the higher-ceiling extension portfolio in parallel.

## Phased plan — what auto-builds vs. what needs you
**Phase 1 — package what exists (wk 1–2 · you: ~4h):** Shay/Claude Code builds
`scripts/marketplace/package-template.js <tag>` → clean dist, README, license, a 1590×1190 preview
screenshot (Puppeteer), ZIP. Run it on your 5–8 cleanest sites (Groove Theory, JJ BA Transport,
Luna's, Crumb & Crust…). **You:** make 1 Creative Market seller account, upload 3–5, price $29–49.

**Phase 2 — automate production (wk 3–6 · you: ~2h):** `batch-produce.js` — niche brief → Site Studio
→ package → `marketplace/ready/`. A `niches.json` of 50 thin-supply verticals. Reuse the content-engine
quality gate. **You:** approve + upload 1–2/wk (the only recurring touchpoint).

**Phase 3 — the upsell (mo 3–6 · you: ~8h):** landing page → Typeform brief → Shay runs Site Studio →
emails the ZIP in 24h → Gumroad/LemonSqueezy payment. No hosting, no SLA, no churn.

**Parallel track (when Phase 1 is banking):** start the Chrome extension portfolio — 5–10 niche
extensions (AI writing, SEO, tab tools), freemium + $5–15/mo via ExtensionPay. Higher ceiling, truest
to your Play-Store vision.

## The minimum non-negotiable effort (even "passive" needs this)
1. Create the accounts — Creative Market seller, Gumroad (agents can't create monetization accounts for you). ~1h
2. Upload the first 5 templates. ~2h
3. Answer first-30-day buyer questions (builds the review signal). ~1h
4. Approve 1–2 new templates/mo. ~30min/mo
Everything else — production, packaging, QA, niche picking, screenshots — is Shay + Claude Code.

## Skip (the swarm killed these honestly)
- **GPT Store / Claude-plugin as *revenue***: discovery broken, monetization not public (most earn $0). Use the plugin only as a *free funnel*.
- **Micro-SaaS subscription**: churn + support + funded competitors (Durable/Wix) = wrong for hands-off.
- **ThemeForest**: wrong format (WordPress, not your HTML) + support SLA. Creative Market wins.
- **Gumroad as a *discovery* channel**: it's checkout only; fine for the $299 upsell, useless for browse traffic.

## So when you wake up
One sentence: **"Shay, package my 5 cleanest existing sites into Creative-Market-ready template ZIPs
and put them in `marketplace/ready/` for me to upload."** That's it. That's the start. The rest waits
its turn — which, per your own read of yourself, is exactly the discipline that changes things.

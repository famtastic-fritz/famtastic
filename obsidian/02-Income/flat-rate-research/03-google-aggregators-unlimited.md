## ⚠️ SCAM WARNING (CISPA "Real Money, Fake Models", Mar 2026)
- **45.83% of "shadow"/reseller unlimited-LLM APIs DON'T run the advertised model** (you pay for GPT-5/Claude, get a 7B open model). 36.95% vs 83.82% accuracy gap.
- Red flags: claims **GPT-5/Claude/Gemini unlimited at deep discount** (no such wholesale flat tier exists); no concurrency/rate-limit docs; no company/ToS; 70–90% cheaper than official.
- **Featherless is the ONE legit unlimited service — and it's open models only** (no frontier). Any "unlimited Claude/GPT for $X" = con.

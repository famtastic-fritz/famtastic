# FAMtastic Designs — Marketing & Positioning

> How we say it, to whom, and why they believe it.
>
> Generated by the Agent Factory · 2026-06-18 · internal use only


## Positioning statement
For small businesses and agencies with a website that looks generic or leaks
revenue, **FAMtastic Designs** builds *confidently-different* sites in days —
proof-driven craft that a normal agency would not ship — without the months-long
agency timeline or the cookie-cutter template look.

## Who we target first (beachhead, in priority order)
1. **Agencies with overflow** — they have demand they can't service; we are the
   white-label factory behind them. Fastest cash, repeat buyers.
2. **Merchants with a live, leaking checkout/funnel** — acute pain, paid
   diagnostic lands easily.
3. **Local service businesses with an embarrassing site** — barber, bakery,
   transport, flowers (the factory already has proof builds in these verticals).

## Message hierarchy
- **Hook (pain):** "A generic site is quietly costing you customers."
- **Proof (craft):** before/after artifacts, Loom walkthroughs, 48h turnaround.
- **Offer (low-risk first step):** the $149 audit / $500 diagnostic.
- **Close (results are the proof):** "If the fixes don't make sense, you keep
  the audit and owe nothing further."

## Proof assets to manufacture (from real deliveries)
- One public-safe **before/after** per completed build.
- A 3–5 min **Loom** narrating the leak found and the fix.
- A one-page **case snapshot** (problem → change → measurable result).
- A reusable **hero gallery** screenshot reel from factory builds.

## Channels (ranked by speed-to-cash, not by reach)
1. **Direct outreach** (email + LinkedIn) — controllable, fast, free.
2. **Upwork / marketplaces** — buyers with budget and intent already there.
3. **Referral loop** — every delivered job asks for one intro.
4. **Content proof** — post each before/after; let the work recruit.
5. **Paid (gated):** small LinkedIn/Google spend behind a "conversion leak
   audit" page — only after the first paid job exists. Retargeting only after
   traffic exists.

## Brand voice
FAMtastic: fearless, specific, unapologetic, proof-over-promises. No hedging, no
"we think we can help." We diagnose, we fix, the result is the argument.

## 30-day marketing calendar (lightweight)
- Wk1: publish offer page + 2 proof artifacts; start outreach.
- Wk2: 3 posts (before/after, the "leak" idea, a client win); referral asks.
- Wk3: turn best-performing message into the outreach default; first case study.
- Wk4: gate-check — if ≥1 paid job, switch on a $10–20/day audit-page test.

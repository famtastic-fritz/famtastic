# AI Automation Service Pitch for NIBS Staff

**Target Audience:** Other NIBS (National Institute of Biomedical Sciences) staffers who have repetitive, manual tasks that could be automated with AI (e.g., data entry, report generation, email sorting, literature review, etc.).

**Service Offering:** AI-Powered Task Automation Consultant
- Identify automation opportunities in daily workflows.
- Design and implement simple AI/automation solutions (using no-code/low-code tools, scripts, or custom integrations).
- Provide training and documentation for staff to maintain and extend the automations.

**Pricing Model:**
- **Discovery Session (Free):** 30-minute call to identify pain points and sketch a solution.
- **Implementation:** $150/hour (estimated 5-20 hours per task depending on complexity).
- **Package Deals:** 
  - Starter Automation: $750 (up to 5 hours, simple task like email filtering or data formatting).
  - Standard Automation: $1,500 (up to 15 hours, moderate task like report generation from multiple sources).
  - Advanced Automation: $3,000+ (custom scope, involves multiple systems or AI model tuning).

**Deliverables:**
1. Process Documentation (current vs. automated).
2. Working Automation (Zapier/Make.com/Python script/etc.).
3. User Guide and Training Session (1 hour).
4. 30-day Support for adjustments.

**Contact Strategy:**
1. **Internal Outreach:** 
   - Send a brief email to NIBS staff mailing list (or known contacts) with a subject line: "Free AI Automation Idea Session – Save 5+ Hours/Week".
   - Include a link to a Calendly scheduler for the free discovery session.
2. **Leverage Existing Networks:**
   - Ask Fritz to introduce you to 3-5 colleagues who have mentioned manual bottlenecks.
   - Offer a referral discount: 10% off for both parties if a referral leads to a paid engagement.
3. **Follow-Up:**
   - After the free session, send a summary email with the proposed solution, estimated hours, and cost.
   - Use a friendly, helpful tone—focus on saving them time, not selling.

**Script for Free Discovery Session (30 minutes):**

**Introduction (5 min):**
- "Thanks for meeting! I’m exploring how AI and automation can help NIBS staff reclaim time from repetitive tasks. I’ll keep this focused: I want to learn about one task that takes you too much time or feels error-prone."

**Task Exploration (15 min):**
- "Walk me through your typical week. What’s a task you do regularly that involves copying data, filling templates, or chasing information?"
- "If you could wave a wand and have that task done automatically, what would you want it to do?"
- "What tools/systems do you currently use for this task? (e.g., Excel, email, specific databases, web portals)"
- "What’s the biggest pain point? (time, mistakes, boredom, waiting on others?)"

**Solution Sketch (8 min):**
- Based on the task, outline a simple automation idea:
  - Example: If they manually compile a weekly report from multiple spreadsheets → propose a script that pulls the data, formats it, and emails the report every Monday.
  - Mention tools you’d use (e.g., "I could set up a Zapier workflow that watches for new files in a folder and generates the report, or write a short Python script if we need more flexibility").
- Emphasize low risk: "We can start with a small prototype to prove it works before investing more time."

**Next Steps & Close (2 min):**
- "If this sounds useful, I can send over a one-page proposal with steps, timeline, and cost. Would you like me to do that?"
- "If not, no worries—I’m happy to have just exchanged ideas. Feel free to reach out if anything else comes up."

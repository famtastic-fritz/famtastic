# AI Automation Pricing for NIBS

## Tiered Pricing

### Starter - $1,500
- One custom AI automation (e.g., auto-summarize meeting notes, generate weekly report)
- Includes: Consultation, setup, testing, 1-hour training
- Delivery: 1 week

### Standard - $3,500
- Three to five connected automations (e.g., grant tracking + email reminders + document filing)
- Includes: Consultation, setup, testing, 2-hour training, 1 month support
- Delivery: 2-3 weeks

### Enterprise - Custom
- Department-wide AI workflow redesign
- Includes: Process audit, custom solution design, deployment, training, ongoing support
- Starting at: $10,000

## Notes
- All prices are one-time setup unless ongoing support is contracted.
- Discounts available for multiple purchases or long-term contracts.

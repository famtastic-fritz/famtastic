# Contact Strategy for Paul (NCS)

## Assumptions
- Paul is likely a decision-maker (IT Manager, Director, or CTO) at NCS.
- We may have prior contact or can find via LinkedIn/company website.

## Steps
1. **Research**: Look up Paul's background on LinkedIn, NCS website.
2. **Warm Introduction**: If possible, get introduced via mutual connection.
3. **Cold Outreach** (if no warm path):
   - Email: Subject line focusing on pain point (e.g., "Reducing NCS maintenance costs by 30%")
   - Body: Brief, value-focused, request 15-minute call.
   - Follow-up: 3 days later if no reply.
4. **Call**: Use pitch deck, focus on outcomes.
5. **Close**: Offer assessment for $5K (or waive fee if leading to larger project).

## Tools
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (if available)
- Email tracking (e.g., HubSpot free)
- Calendar link (Calendly) for easy scheduling
